Portraits


One of Laura's greatest passions as a photographer is to create portraits that are surprising and portray the depths of the subject's soul. Sincerity and playfulness have been the main themes of Laura's career as a portrait photographer. In addition to this, Laura is interested in expressing the raw power of femininity.
