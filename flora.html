Flora of Finland


Laura worked at Flora of Finland as an entrepreneur with her business partner in 2016-2018.
In cooperation with her business partner, Laura made Flora of Finland's portraits of Finnish wild plants. In this collaboration, Laura worked as a photographer and image retoucher.

Flora of Finland released products for sale, such as limited edition art posters, glass prints and linen towels. The products had retailers all over Finland with an emphasis on art museum stores. Flora of Finland was featured in various art and design publications and exhibitions, also in Japan.
