Curated Group Exhibitions


FIN/100 Pop Up Exhibition

ARTEK, 21_21 Design Sight
Tokyo, Japan
2017

In 2017, Finland celebrated 100 years as an independent nation with continuous democracy. To commemorate the centenary, Artek, Iittala/ARABIA, Johanna Gullichsen, Kauniste Finland, LUMI, Lapuan Kankurit and Flora of Finland, these Finnish design brands joined hands to hold a pop up project in Tokyo. FIN/100 brought together the best Finnish design and showcased it in the full spectrum.
